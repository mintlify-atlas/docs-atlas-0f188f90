This document outlines potential improvements and enhancements for the Neutronium compiler and its toolchain. These improvements aim to make the compiler more robust, efficient, and user-friendly.

<Note>
This is a forward-looking document about potential compiler enhancements. Priorities and implementation timeline are subject to change.
</Note>
